Challenge Protocol

Elite bots

  • 2300+ rating accepted in rated games.
  • Standard and Chess960 are supported.
  • Time controls from bullet to classical are allowed.

Protection rules

  • Low-rated bots are routed to safer casual games.
  • Opponent repeat limits protect rating and variety.
  • Matchmaking can enter protection mode after rating drops.

Core Capabilities

Engine stack

  • Ethereal-based UCI engine pipeline.
  • NNUE evaluation with opening book support.
  • Safer time management for increment games.

Operations

  • Auto-abort for inactive opponents.
  • Fallback move selection if the engine fails.
  • Optional chat and post-game messaging.
